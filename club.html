ESM Program

Excellence in Student Management

About ESM

The ESM (Excellence in Student Management) program is designed to develop leadership and management skills among our members through hands-on experience and structured learning.

Program Benefits

Participants gain practical experience in project management, team leadership, event organization, and strategic planning while contributing to the club's success.

How to Join

The ESM program accepts applications at the beginning of each semester. Candidates go through a selection process that includes interviews and assessment activities.
